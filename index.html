Products

Flake System

The Flake System is a versatile and highly durable epoxy flooring solution that adds a decorative touch to your residential, commercial, or industrial spaces. It consists of multi-colored flakes embedded in a high-performance epoxy resin, creating a seamless, slip-resistant surface. Ideal for garages, basements, showrooms, or retail spaces, the Flake System is easy to clean and maintain, offering excellent resistance to chemicals, abrasions, and impacts. Choose from a wide variety of flake colors and sizes to customize the appearance of your floor, and enjoy a stylish, long-lasting surface that enhances the aesthetics and functionality of any space.

Quartz System

The Quartz System is a high-quality epoxy flooring solution that combines the strength of epoxy resins with the beauty of natural quartz aggregates. This durable, seamless surface provides exceptional performance in both residential and commercial settings, including kitchens, bathrooms, offices, and warehouses. The Quartz System offers superior resistance to chemicals, impacts, and heavy foot traffic while providing a slip-resistant, hygienic surface that is easy to clean and maintain. With a wide range of color options and finishes available, you can create a stunning, one-of-a-kind floor that matches your unique design preferences and adds value to your property.
